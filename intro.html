Introduction

Information Assurance Security (IAS) relates to the measures and processes implemented to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, availability, authentication, and non-repudiation of information and information systems. It involves safeguarding sensitive data from unauthorized access, destruction, or alteration while ensuring that authorized users can access the information when needed.
